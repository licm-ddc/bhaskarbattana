About Us

Founder Profile

Pastor Bhaskar Battana was born in a hindu padmashali caste, and his wife Kavitha was born in hindu Brahmin caste and both accepted Jesus as their Lord and saviour as youngsters. They were married in the year 1996 and are blessed with two daughters, Keerthi and Kruthi.

Pastor Bhaskar and his wife Kavitha came to the full time ministry in the year 2002 in response to God’s call for their lives. Because they come from a hindu background, they have started their ministry journey all alone, they do not have any support from any organisation or from people, literally facing many struggles in terms of money, people and oppositions and yet supported by God alone. Like Abraham, God alone was their provider from the beginning and even now. They have started the Church in the year 2007 with a passion to win souls for God’s kingdom and lead people towards true character of God. Pastor Bhaskar actively moves in the gifts of the Holy Spirt. Especially, in the area of healing, prophetic, and deliverance ministry. He passionately preaches the gospel of Jesus wherever he goes.

We Believe

We believe that in the beginning God created the universe and man and three persons are involved in the creation of the world and man…God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit. (Phil.2:5-7; John 15:26; 1Pet.1:2).

We believe that Jesus is the Son of God and He came to the world in the flesh (1John 4:2) to die on the cross by shedding His precious blood on the cross and rose on the third day (1Cor.15:3-4) for the remission of the sins of the world.

We believe that whosoever believes in Jesus Christ will be saved and possess a place in heaven.

We believe in the water baptism, presence of the Holy Spirit and His gifts, like speaking in tongues, in the miracle, healings and wonders in the mighty name of Jesus. (Math.28:19;Acts 2:2-4;Dan.4:3; Isa.53:3-5)

We believe that we are saved by grace through faith in Lord Jesus Christ. (Eph.2:8; John 14:6).

We belive in the second coming of Jesus and the final trumphet sound to take His Church to heaven where there will be an eternal existence of the children of God. (1Thess.4:16-17; Acts 1:1).

Vision

To bring the people into the image and likeness of God. (2Cor.3:18; Col.3:10).

To reach out to the unsaved. (Mark 16:15).

To prepare the Church to move in faith and to meet the Lord in the air to be with Him forever. (1Thess.4:17).

Faith Statement

we have started our journey by faith and still walking by faith and we will continue to do the ministry by faith.
